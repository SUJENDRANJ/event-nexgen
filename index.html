RIVIERA 2025 - GMKMC Sports Meet

ZAPHYRONZ presents the 4th edition of RIVIERA: A legacy of unity, competition, and celebration at Government Mohan Kumaramangalam Medical College (GMKMC).

About RIVIERA

RIVIERA is more than a sports meet — it is a legacy that started in 2012 by the Batch of 2008 and became a milestone in GMKMC history.

After an eight-year pause due to the pandemic, it returns with renewed vigor, representing Resilience, Reunion, and Revival.

RIVIERA highlights athletic excellence, team spirit, and camaraderie among students from various colleges. This year, we proudly host a range of intercollegiate competitions showcasing discipline, determination, and friendship.

General Rules

  • Registration is compulsory. Only MBBS students from batches 2020 to 2025 can participate.
  • Participants must present college ID cards and two passport size photos to receive a delegate pass.
  • Teams must carry proof of registration fee payment; delegate passes must be shown before each match.
  • Report 30 minutes prior to the match; schedules are communicated one day in advance.
  • Decisions by referees and organizing committee are final.
  • Smoking, alcohol, and narcotics are strictly prohibited on campus and will lead to disqualification and possible legal action.
  • Damage to college property will result in forfeiture.
